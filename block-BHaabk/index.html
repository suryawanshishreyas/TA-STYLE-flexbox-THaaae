About Valorant Agents

Agents have one Signature Ability which they get for free each round

Agents have a base health of 100; Light Shields bring the maximum health to 125, and Heavy Shields to 150. It is the intention of the developers that one agent be released per act.

Each agent fulfills one of four roles, defined by their abilities and playstyle: 1.Controller, 2.Duelists, 3. Sentinel, 4.Initiators.
